About Sainik School East Siang

Everything you need to know about SSES Niglok, Arunachal Pradesh

Sainik School East Siang (SSES) is a prestigious residential school located in Niglok village, Ruksin, East Siang district of Arunachal Pradesh. Established in 2018, it is one of the newest additions to the network of 33+ Sainik Schools across India.

The school is managed by the Sainik Schools Society under the Ministry of Defence, Government of India. It is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) with affiliation number 228005.

Sainik School East Siang provides quality education along with military orientation to prepare students for entry into the National Defence Academy (NDA), Indian Naval Academy, and other officer training academies. The school admits students from Class VI onwards.

How to Get Admission?

Admission to Sainik School East Siang is through the All India Sainik Schools Entrance Examination (AISSEE) conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA). Here's the step-by-step process:

📝 Step-by-Step Admission Process

  1. Step 1: Visit NTA website (exams.nta.nic.in/sainik-school-society)
  2. Step 2: Register and fill online application form
  3. Step 3: Upload required documents (photo, signature, certificates)
  4. Step 4: Pay application fee (₹850 for General, ₹700 for SC/ST)
  5. Step 5: Download admit card before exam
  6. Step 6: Appear for AISSEE exam (January 2027)
  7. Step 7: Check results on NTA website
  8. Step 8: Attend medical test if shortlisted
  9. Step 9: Complete document verification
  10. Step 10: Pay fees and confirm admission
