About Sainik School East Siang

Gateway to NDA, INA & Indian Armed Forces

Sainik School East Siang (SSES) was raised on 27 August 2018 as the 28th Sainik School of India. It is a residential school for Boys & Girls (Cadets) located in Niglok village, near the banks of Sille River, in the East Siang district of Arunachal Pradesh.

The school is spread over 100 acres and provides public school education along with military orientation. The aim is to prepare young boys and girls to join Defence Services, namely National Defence Academy (NDA), Indian Naval Academy (INA), and all other military academies.

The school motto is "Education, Character, Nationalism" - reflecting its commitment to holistic development of students.

How to Reach

  • By Road: 6 km from Ruksin, near Arunachal-Assam border
  • By Rail: Murkongselek railway station (10 km from school)
  • By Air: Pasighat Airport (35 km from school)
  • Taxi: Private taxi services available for commuting

How to Get Admission?

Admission to Sainik School East Siang is through the All India Sainik Schools Entrance Examination (AISSEE) conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA). Here's the step-by-step process:

📝 Step-by-Step Admission Process

  1. Step 1: Visit NTA website (exams.nta.nic.in/sainik-school-society)
  2. Step 2: Register and fill online application form
  3. Step 3: Upload required documents (photo, signature, certificates)
  4. Step 4: Pay application fee (₹850 for General, ₹700 for SC/ST)
  5. Step 5: Download admit card before exam
  6. Step 6: Appear for AISSEE exam (January 2027)
  7. Step 7: Check results on NTA website
  8. Step 8: Attend medical test if shortlisted
  9. Step 9: Complete document verification
  10. Step 10: Pay fees and confirm admission
