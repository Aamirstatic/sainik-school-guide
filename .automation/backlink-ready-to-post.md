### Answer 1: For "How to prepare for Sainik School entrance exam?"

```
Preparing for AISSEE requires a structured approach. Here's what works:

**Mathematics (150 marks for Class 6):** Focus on NCERT first. 80% of questions come from NCERT-level concepts. Key chapters: Mensuration, LCM/HCF, Ratio, Percentage. Use R.S. Aggarwal for extra practice.

**GK (50 marks):** Lucent's GK covers static portion. For current affairs, read newspaper daily or use a GK app. 6-8 questions come from current affairs every year.

**Language (50 marks):** Practice one comprehension passage daily. Wren & Martin for grammar.

**Intelligence (50 marks):** R.S. Aggarwal Reasoning. Practice 15 questions daily. Patterns: Series, Coding-Decoding, Mirror Image.

Important: There's NO negative marking in AISSEE. Attempt every question.
```
