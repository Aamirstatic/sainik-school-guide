# AISSEE 2027 - Complete Guide

All India Sainik School Entrance Exam (AISSEE) is conducted by National Testing Agency (NTA) for admission to Class 6 and Class 9 in 33+ Sainik Schools across India.

## 📋 Quick Overview

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Full Form** | All India Sainik School Entrance Exam |
| **Conducting Body** | National Testing Agency (NTA) |
| **Exam Level** | National |
| **Frequency** | Once a year |
| **Mode** | Pen and Paper (OMR based) |
| **Medium** | Hindi, English, and Regional Languages |
| **Classes** | Class 6 and Class 9 |

## ✅ Eligibility Criteria

### For Class 6
- **Age:** 10-12 years as on March 31 of admission year
- **Qualification:** Currently studying in Class 5 or equivalent
- **Nationality:** Indian citizen

### For Class 9
- **Age:** 13-15 years as on March 31 of admission year
- **Qualification:** Currently studying in Class 8 or equivalent
- **Nationality:** Indian citizen

### Reservation
- **SC:** 15%
- **ST:** 7.5%
- **OBC:** 27%
- **Defence:** 25% (children of serving/ex-defence personnel)
- **Home State:** 67% seats for domicile students

## 📖 How to Apply

### Step-by-Step Process

1. **Visit Official Website**
   - Go to exams.nta.nic.in/sainik-school-society

2. **Register as New User**
   - Click on "New Registration"
   - Fill basic details
   - Create login credentials

3. **Fill Application Form**
   - Personal details
   - Academic details
   - Choice of Sainik Schools (up to 3 preferences)
   - Upload documents:
     - Passport size photo
     - Signature
     - Birth certificate
     - Domicile certificate

4. **Pay Application Fee**
   - General/OBC: ₹550
   - SC/ST: ₹400
   - Payment mode: Online (Credit/Debit Card/Net Banking)

5. **Submit and Print**
   - Review all details
   - Submit application
   - Download and print confirmation page

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Is there negative marking in AISSEE?
Yes, there is negative marking. For Class 6, 1 mark is deducted for wrong answers. For Class 9, 1 mark is deducted for wrong answers.

### Can girls apply for AISSEE?
Yes, from 2021 onwards, girls can also apply for admission to Class 6 in Sainik Schools.

### How many times can I attempt AISSEE?
There is no limit on the number of attempts, as long as you meet the age criteria.

### Can I choose multiple Sainik Schools?
Yes, you can choose up to 3 Sainik Schools in order of preference while filling the application form.

### What if I miss the application deadline?
You cannot apply after the deadline. Keep checking the official website for notification dates and apply well in advance.
