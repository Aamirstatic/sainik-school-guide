## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. Can you help me with my AISSEE application form?

We provide detailed guides on how to fill the AISSEE application form, but we cannot process applications on your behalf. Please visit the official NTA portal at [exams.nta.ac.in/AISSEE](https://exams.nta.ac.in/AISSEE) to submit your application.

### 2. I found incorrect information on your website. How do I report it?

Please email us at [info@sainikschooleastsiang.in](mailto:info@sainikschooleastsiang.in) with the specific page URL and the incorrect information. We verify and correct all reported errors within 48 hours.

### 3. Do you offer personal AISSEE coaching or tutoring?

No. We are an information portal, not a coaching institute. We recommend preparing through NCERT textbooks, previous year papers, and dedicated AISSEE preparation books. See our [AISSEE preparation guide](/aissee/) for detailed study plans.

### 4. How can I get admission in Sainik School East Siang specifically?

Admission to Sainik School East Siang (and all Sainik Schools) is through the All India Sainik School Entrance Exam (AISSEE). You need to apply through the NTA portal, appear for the exam, and qualify in the merit list. Visit our [AISSEE guide](/aissee/) for complete details.
