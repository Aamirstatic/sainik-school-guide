## Frequently Asked Questions

Before contacting us, check if your question is answered here:

### Is this an official Sainik School website?
No. We are an independent educational resource. For official information, visit [sainikschoolsociety.in](https://sainikschoolsociety.in) or [exams.nta.nic.in](https://exams.nta.nic.in/sainik-school-society/).

### Can you help with Sainik School admission?
We provide information and guidance through our articles. We cannot directly help with admissions. For official admission queries, contact the specific Sainik School or NTA.

### How often is the content updated?
We update our content regularly, especially during the admission season (September-April). Fee structures and admission dates are updated as soon as official notifications are released.

### Can I contribute articles?
Yes. If you have expertise in defense education, Sainik School admissions, or AISSEE preparation, email us at info@sainikschooleastsiang.in with your article idea.

### Do you provide coaching?
We do not provide coaching. We recommend [JGPS Coaching](https://jgps.in/) for AISSEE preparation.

## Report an Error

Found incorrect information on our website? Please email us at info@sainikschooleastsiang.in with:
- The page URL where you found the error
- The incorrect information
- The correct information (if you know it)
- A source link (if available)
