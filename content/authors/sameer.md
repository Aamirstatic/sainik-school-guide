# About Sameer

Sameer is an education researcher at Sainik School Guide India. He has spent over 6 years studying how students perform in competitive entrance exams like AISSEE, and what patterns separate successful candidates from the rest.

His background is in data analysis. Before joining Sainik School Guide, he worked with coaching institutes in Bihar and Uttar Pradesh, tracking student performance across multiple batches. That experience gave him a practical understanding of what works and what doesn't in exam preparation.

## What He Does

Sameer's work focuses on three areas:

**Exam Pattern Analysis** - He breaks down AISSEE question papers from previous years, identifying which topics carry the most weight and how difficulty levels have changed over time. His analysis helps students focus on what actually matters.

**Admission Data Tracking** - He monitors cutoff trends, seat allocation patterns, and state-wise admission statistics across all 33+ Sainik Schools. Parents use his data to set realistic targets.

**Student Performance Research** - He studies what daily habits, study routines, and preparation strategies correlate with selection. Not theory, but actual data from students who made it.

## Writing Approach

Sameer writes with numbers first. Every claim he makes is backed by data, whether it's from official NTA reports, school websites, or his own research. He avoids vague advice like "study hard" and instead gives specific targets: how many hours, which chapters, what score to aim for.

His articles include tables, comparison charts, and step-by-step breakdowns because that's how he thinks. Parents tell him the data-heavy approach helps them track their child's progress objectively.
