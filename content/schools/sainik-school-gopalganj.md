# Sainik School Gopalganj

Sainik School Gopalganj Bihar ke ek premier Sainik School hai, Gopalganj district mein sthit. Yeh school Ministry of Defence ke under Sainik Schools Society dwara operate hota hai aur CBSE se affiliated hai. Bihar mein do Sainik Schools hain — ek Gopalganj aur doosra Nalanda. School ka mission hai young cadets ko NDA aur defence services ke liye tayyar karna aur unhe ek disciplined aur academic environment provide karna.

## 🎯 Admission Process

### Eligibility Criteria (पात्रता मानदंड)

**For Class 6 (कक्षा 6 के लिए):**
- Age (आयु): 10-12 years as on March 31 of admission year
- Class (कक्षा): Currently studying in Class 5
- Exam (परीक्षा): AISSEE conducted by NTA

**For Class 9 (कक्षा 9 के लिए):**
- Age (आयु): 13-15 years as on March 31 of admission year
- Class (कक्षा): Currently studying in Class 8
- Exam (परीक्षा): AISSEE conducted by NTA

### Admission Steps (प्रवेश प्रक्रिया)

1. **Apply Online (ऑनलाइन आवेदन)** through NTA website (exams.nta.nic.in/sainik-school-society)
2. **Appear for AISSEE (AISSEE परीक्षा दें)** — usually held in January
3. **Check Results (परिणाम जांचें)** on NTA website
4. **Medical Test (मेडिकल टेस्ट)** for shortlisted candidates
5. **Final Merit List (अंतिम मेरिट सूची)** based on AISSEE score + medical fitness
6. **Spot Admission (स्पॉट एडमिशन)** — Offline counselling for remaining seats

### Important Dates (Tentative for 2027)

- **Application Start:** October 2026
- **Last Date:** November 2026
- **Exam Date:** January 2027
- **Results:** February 2027
- **Medical Test:** March 2027
- **Session Start:** April 2027

### Bihar Domicile Reservation

Bihar ke students ko **67% seats reserved** hain. Baaki 33% seats other states ke students ke liye hain. Iska matlab hai ki Bihar ke students ke liye admission relatively easier hai.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (अक्सर पूछे जाने वाले सवाल)

### Sainik School Gopalganj mein admission kaise lein?
Admission AISSEE (All India Sainik School Entrance Exam) ke through hota hai jo NTA conduct karti hai. Students ko online apply karna hota hai, exam dena hota hai, medical test clear karna hota hai, aur phir merit ke basis par admission milta hai. Spot admission rounds bhi hote hain remaining seats ke liye.

### Kya Bihar ke students ko reservation milta hai?
Haan, 67% seats Bihar domicile students ke liye reserved hain. Baaki 33% seats other states ke students ke liye hain.

### Medium of instruction kya hai?
Medium of instruction English hai. Hindi compulsory language ke roop mein padhai jaati hai.

### Kya girls bhi apply kar sakti hain?
Haan, 2021 se girls bhi Class 6 mein admission ke liye apply kar sakti hain. School ab co-education mode mein hai.

### Sainik School Gopalganj ki fees kitni hai?
Annual fees approximately ₹2,17,000 hai. SC/ST students ko up to 50% fee concession milta hai. Defence personnel ke bachchon ke liye bhi special discount hai.

### Bihar mein kitne Sainik Schools hain?
Bihar mein **do Sainik Schools** hain:
1. Sainik School Gopalganj (established 1964)
2. Sainik School Nalanda (established 2003)

Dono hi reputed schools hain aur NDA ke liye students tayyar karte hain.

### Sainik School ke baad kya career options hain?
Students join kar sakte hain:
- National Defence Academy (NDA)
- Indian Military Academy (IMA)
- Naval Academy
- Air Force Academy
- Civil services
- Engineering and medical fields
