## History and Foundation

### The Vision Behind Sainik School Kapurthala

Sainik School Kapurthala was established as the seventh school in the nationwide Sainik Schools network, conceived by the Ministry of Defence to prepare young boys academically, physically, and mentally for entry into the National Defence Academy (NDA) at Khadakwasla, Pune, and the Indian Naval Academy at Ezhimala, Kerala. The school was inaugurated on 8 July 1961 by the then Defence Minister of India, V. K. Krishna Menon, marking a significant milestone in India's defence education infrastructure.

The Sainik Schools were a direct response to the lessons of the 1962 conflict and the broader need to democratise officer-level recruitment into the Indian Armed Forces. Sainik School Kapurthala became the vehicle through which youth from Punjab and northern India could access structured military preparatory education.

### The Jagatjit Palace — A Living Heritage

The school occupies the historic Jagatjit Palace, built in 1908 by Maharaja Jagatjit Singh of Kapurthala. The palace is an extraordinary example of French Renaissance architecture, directly inspired by the Palace of Versailles during the reign of Louis XIV. The Indo-French architectural style features ornate facades, symmetrical gardens, and grand interiors that have been adapted for educational use.

The total campus area measures 1961 kanals and 18 marlas (approximately 245 acres), making it one of the most spacious Sainik School campuses in India. The palace building continues to serve as the administrative and academic hub, while modern facilities including laboratories, hostels, and sports infrastructure have been developed within the campus grounds.

A notable memorial within the campus is **Saikap Smrithi Sthal**, a tribute to alumni who made the supreme sacrifice in service of the nation.

## Academics and Curriculum

### CBSE Affiliation and Academic Structure

Sainik School Kapurthala is permanently affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi. The school follows the CBSE curriculum from Class VI through Class XII, with English as the medium of instruction.

The academic programme is designed to provide a strong foundation in:

- **Science and Mathematics** — Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics at the senior secondary level
- **Social Sciences** — History, Geography, Political Science, and Economics
- **Languages** — English (compulsory), Hindi, and Punjabi
- **Computer Science** — Integrated into the curriculum from middle school onwards

### Academic Calendar and Assessment

The academic session runs from April to March, following the CBSE assessment pattern:

- **Periodic Tests (PT-1, PT-2)** — Conducted quarterly
- **Half-Yearly Examinations** — Mid-session assessment
- **Annual/Final Examinations** — End-of-session board pattern exams
- **Pre-Board Examinations** — For Class X and Class XII students ahead of CBSE board exams

### NDA and Defence Preparation

The primary aim of Sainik School Kapurthala is to prepare cadets for entry into the National Defence Academy. The school provides dedicated coaching for the NDA entrance examination, which is conducted by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC). In addition to academic preparation, cadets undergo systematic physical training, personality development, and leadership grooming that align with the Services Selection Board (SSB) interview process.

## Admission Process

### AISSEE — All India Sainik Schools Entrance Examination

Admission to Sainik School Kapurthala is through the All India Sainik Schools Entrance Examination (AISSEE), conducted annually by the National Testing Agency (NTA).

**Eligibility Criteria:**

- **Class VI:** Boys and girls aged 10–12 years as on 31 March of the admission year
- **Class IX:** Boys aged 13–15 years as on 31 March of the admission year (against vacant seats)

**Seat Reservation:**

- 67% seats are reserved for domicile candidates of Punjab
- 33% seats are open to candidates from other states
- Category-wise reservations as per Government of India norms: Scheduled Caste (15%), Scheduled Tribe (7.5%), Other Backward Classes (27%), and Defence (25%)

### Admission Procedure

1. **Online Registration** — Through the NTA website at exams.nta.nic.in/sainik-school-society
2. **Entrance Examination** — Held on the first Sunday of January each year
3. **Result Declaration** — Merit list published by NTA
4. **Medical Examination** — Shortlisted candidates undergo medical fitness tests
5. **Counselling and Document Verification** — Final admission after verification of documents

### Fee Structure

The annual fee for Sainik School Kapurthala is approximately ₹1,00,000 to ₹1,40,000 per year, which includes tuition, boarding, lodging, and messing charges. Scholarships are available from the Ministry of Defence and the Government of Punjab for eligible candidates.

Inter-house competitions in academics, sports, debates, and cultural activities are held throughout the year.
