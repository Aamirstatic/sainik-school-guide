## How Sainik School Age Limit Works

The Sainik Schools Society sets the age criteria for AISSEE admissions. The age is calculated as on **March 31 of the admission year**, not the exam date. This is important because the exam happens in January, but the age cutoff is March 31.

### Class 6 Age Limit (AISSEE 2027)

| Criteria | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| **Minimum Age** | 10 years |
| **Maximum Age** | 12 years |
| **Age Date** | As on March 31, 2027 |
| **Born Between** | April 1, 2015 to March 31, 2017 |
| **Current Class** | Studying in Class 5 |

### Class 9 Age Limit (AISSEE 2027)

| Criteria | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| **Minimum Age** | 13 years |
| **Maximum Age** | 15 years |
| **Age Date** | As on March 31, 2027 |
| **Born Between** | April 1, 2012 to March 31, 2014 |
| **Current Class** | Studying in Class 8 |

If your child's date of birth falls within these ranges, they are eligible. If it falls outside by even one day, the application will be rejected.

## Age Relaxation for SC/ST/OBC Candidates

The Government of India provides age relaxation for reserved category students. This is applicable to all Sainik Schools.

### Category-wise Age Relaxation

| Category | Age Relaxation | Effective Upper Age |
|----------|---------------|-------------------|
| **General** | No relaxation | Class 6: 12 years, Class 9: 15 years |
| **SC (Scheduled Caste)** | 2 years | Class 6: 14 years, Class 9: 17 years |
| **ST (Scheduled Tribe)** | 2 years | Class 6: 14 years, Class 9: 17 years |
| **OBC-NCL** | No relaxation (central rule) | Same as General |
| **Defence Ward** | No age relaxation | Same as General |

### What This Means

If your child is born on February 28, 2015, and you are applying for Class 6 in 2027:

- **General category:** Age on March 31, 2027 = 12 years, 1 month. Eligible (within 10-12 range).
- **SC/ST category:** Age on March 31, 2027 = 12 years, 1 month. Eligible (upper limit is 14 years for SC/ST).

If your child is born on January 15, 2015:

- **General category:** Age on March 31, 2027 = 12 years, 2.5 months. NOT eligible (exceeds 12 years).
- **SC/ST category:** Age on March 31, 2027 = 12 years, 2.5 months. Eligible (upper limit is 14 years for SC/ST).

**Important:** Age relaxation is only for the upper age limit. The minimum age (10 for Class 6, 13 for Class 9) remains the same for all categories.

## Common Age-Related Questions Parents Ask

### My child is 9 years and 11 months. Can I apply for Class 6?

No. The minimum age is 10 years as on March 31. If your child is 9 years and 11 months on that date, they are not eligible. Wait for the next year.

### My child will turn 13 on April 5, 2027. Is Class 6 still possible?

No. The age cutoff is March 31. If your child turns 13 on April 5, they are already 12 years and 361 days on March 31, which is within the 10-12 range. They are eligible for Class 6 in 2027. But if they turn 13 before March 31, they are not eligible.

### Can I apply for both Class 6 and Class 9?

No. You can apply for only one class per year. Choose based on your child's current class and age eligibility.

### What if my child is eligible for both Class 6 and Class 9?

This is rare but possible if the child is 13-15 years old and currently in Class 8. In this case, apply for Class 9 since it has higher value (direct path to NDA after Class 12).

### Does the age relaxation apply to all Sainik Schools?

Yes. SC/ST age relaxation is applicable to all 33+ Sainik Schools and 69 new Sainik Schools under the PPP model.

### What documents prove age?

| **Aadhaar Card** | Accepted. DOB on Aadhaar is used. |
| **Passport** | Accepted. Valid DOB proof. |

## What If My Child Is Not Eligible This Year?

If the calculator shows your child is not eligible for AISSEE 2027, do not worry. Here are your options:

### Option 1: Wait for Next Year

If your child is too young, they will be eligible next year. Use this time to prepare. Students who start preparing early score much better than those who start late.

### Option 2: Apply for a Different Class

If your child is too old for Class 6 but eligible for Class 9, apply for Class 9. The preparation is different, but the opportunity is the same.

### Option 3: Consider Other Schools

If your child has crossed the age limit for Sainik Schools, consider:
- **Rashtriya Military Schools (RMS)** — Similar age criteria
- **RIMC Dehradun** — Different age window (11.5-13 years)
- **New Sainik Schools (PPP model)** — Same age criteria but more seats

### Option 4: Direct NDA Entry After Class 12

Students can appear for the NDA exam directly after Class 12, even without attending Sainik School. Many officers have taken this path.
