## Punjab Sainik School - Admission Process 2026

Punjab ke Sainik School mein admission ke liye yeh steps follow karein:

### Step 1: AISSEE Registration
- NTA ki official website par online registration karein
- [AISSEE 2026 ki complete details yahaan dekhein](/aissee/)
- Application form carefully bharein
- Required documents upload karein
- Application fee online pay karein

### Step 2: AISSEE Entrance Exam
- Exam January/February mein conduct hota hai
- **Class VI Exam Pattern:**
  - Mathematics - 50 questions
  - General Knowledge - 25 questions
  - Language - 25 questions
  - Intelligence - 25 questions
- **Class IX Exam Pattern:**
  - Mathematics - 50 questions
  - Science - 50 questions
  - English - 25 questions
  - Social Studies - 25 questions

### Step 3: Medical Test
- Written exam pass karne ke baad medical examination
- Physical fitness assessment
- Vision, hearing, aur overall health check

### Step 4: Document Verification
- Birth certificate / Matriculation certificate
- Punjab domicile certificate
- Caste certificate (SC/ST/OBC - if applicable)
- Transfer certificate
- Character certificate
- Passport size photographs

### Step 5: Counselling & Admission
- Merit list ke basis par counselling
- School allotment
- Fee payment aur admission confirmation
