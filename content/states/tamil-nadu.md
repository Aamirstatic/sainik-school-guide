## Tamil Nadu Sainik School - Admission Process 2026

Tamil Nadu ke Sainik School mein admission lene ke liye yeh process follow karein:

### Step 1: AISSEE Online Registration
- National Testing Agency (NTA) ki website par registration karein
- [AISSEE 2026 ki complete information yahaan dekhein](/aissee/)
- Online form bharein, documents upload karein, aur application fee pay karein

### Step 2: AISSEE Entrance Exam
- Exam har saal January/February mein hota hai
- **Class VI ke liye:**
  - Mathematics - 50 marks
  - General Knowledge - 25 marks
  - Language - 25 marks
  - Intelligence - 25 marks
- **Class IX ke liye:**
  - Mathematics - 50 marks
  - Science - 50 marks
  - English - 25 marks
  - Social Studies - 25 marks

### Step 3: Medical Examination
- Written exam qualify karne ke baad medical test hota hai
- Physical fitness aur health check-up
- Vision test, hearing test, general health

### Step 4: Document Verification
- Birth certificate (Matriculation certificate)
- Domicile certificate of Tamil Nadu
- Caste certificate (SC/ST/OBC - if applicable)
- Transfer certificate from previous school
- Passport size photographs

### Step 5: Final Merit List & Admission
- Written exam marks + medical test ke basis par merit list
- Counselling ke through school allotment
- Fee payment aur admission confirmation
