# Sainik Schools in Jammu & Kashmir

Jammu & Kashmir has 2 Sainik Schools. All schools are CBSE-affiliated, fully residential, and admit students through AISSEE conducted by NTA.

## Jammu & Kashmir ke Sainik Schools

| School | Location | Established | Board |
|--------|----------|-------------|-------|
| Sainik School Nagrota | [Nagrota](/schools/sainik-school-nagrota/) | 1970 | CBSE |
| Sainik School Manasbal | [Manasbal](/schools/sainik-school-mansasbal/) | N/A | CBSE |

## Admission Process

Admission in all Sainik Schools is through **AISSEE (All India Sainik School Entrance Exam)** conducted by NTA.

- **Class 6:** Age 10-12 years, currently in Class 5
- **Class 9:** Age 13-15 years, currently in Class 8
- **Application:** Online at exams.nta.nic.in
- **Exam:** Usually held in January
- **67% seats** reserved for home state students
