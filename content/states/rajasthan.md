## Rajasthan Sainik Schools - Admission Process 2026

Rajasthan ke Sainik Schools mein admission lene ke liye yeh steps follow karein:

### Step 1: Online Registration
- NTA ki official website par jaakar register karein
- [AISSEE 2026 ki complete details yahaan dekhein](/aissee/)
- Application form bharein aur fees pay karein

### Step 2: AISSEE Entrance Exam
- Exam January/February mein hota hai
- Class VI ke liye: OMR based exam (Maths, GK, Language, Intelligence)
- Class IX ke liye: Maths, Science, English, Social Studies

### Step 3: Medical Test
- Written exam pass karne ke baad medical test hota hai
- Physical fitness check compulsory hai

### Step 4: Document Verification
- Birth certificate
- Domicile certificate
- Caste certificate (if applicable)
- Previous school transfer certificate

### Step 5: Final Merit List
- Written exam + medical test ke marks ke basis par merit list banti hai
