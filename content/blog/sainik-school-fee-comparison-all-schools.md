## Sainik School Fees 2027: ₹2-2.5 Lakh Per Year — Here Is Where Every Rupee Goes

**Sainik School fees for 2027 range between ₹2 lakh to ₹2.5 lakh per year across all 33+ schools in India.** This makes Sainik Schools significantly cheaper than private boarding schools (₹5-12 lakh/year) but more expensive than government schools. The fee covers tuition, hostel, mess, uniform, books, and medical facilities — essentially everything your child needs for a year of residential education.

> **Key Takeaway:** Sainik School annual fees are approximately ₹2-2.5 lakh. SC/ST students get up to 50% fee reduction. Defence personnel children get special concessions. State government scholarships can further reduce the cost. Payment is made through SBI Collect portal.

Sainik Schools offer multiple scholarship and fee concession schemes. This is where many families save significantly.

### 1. Ministry of Defence Scholarships

Children of serving and ex-servicemen are eligible for fee concessions from the Ministry of Defence.

| Category | Fee Concession |
|---|---|
| Serving Defence Personnel | Up to 50% tuition fee waiver |
| Ex-Servicemen (retired) | Up to 25% tuition fee waiver |
| War Widows / Gallantry Awardees | Up to 75% fee waiver |

## How to Pay Sainik School Fees

All Sainik Schools accept fee payment through **SBI Collect** (State Bank of India's online payment portal). Here is the process:

### Step-by-Step Payment Guide

1. **Visit SBI Collect:** Go to [onlinesbi.sbi.bank.in/sbicollect/icollecthome.htm](https://onlinesbi.sbi.bank.in/sbicollect/icollecthome.htm)
2. **Select State:** Choose the state where the Sainik School is located
3. **Select Institution:** Choose "Sainik School [School Name]"
4. **Enter Details:** Student name, class, admission number
5. **Select Fee Type:** Tuition fee / Hostel fee / Mess charges (as applicable)
6. **Pay:** Credit card, debit card, net banking, or UPI
7. **Save Receipt:** Download and print the payment receipt

**Important:** Most schools have a fee payment deadline (usually May-June for the new session). Late payment may attract a fine or the student may not be allowed to join the hostel.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What is the total annual fee for Sainik School 2027?
The total annual fee for Sainik School in 2027 is approximately ₹2 lakh to ₹2.5 lakh. This includes tuition, hostel, mess, uniform, books, and medical charges. Fees vary slightly by school.

### Is Sainik School cheaper than private boarding schools?
Yes. Sainik Schools charge ₹2-2.5 lakh per year, while private boarding schools charge ₹5-12 lakh per year. Sainik Schools offer similar or better facilities (residential, sports, NCC) at a fraction of the cost.

### Do SC/ST students get fee concession in Sainik School?
Yes. SC/ST students get up to 50% fee reduction in Sainik Schools. OBC students may get up to 25% reduction depending on the state. Apply through the National Scholarship Portal at scholarships.gov.in.

### How do I pay Sainik School fees online?
Sainik School fees are paid through SBI Collect portal. Visit onlinesbi.sbi.bank.in, select the school, enter student details, and pay via credit/debit card, net banking, or UPI.

### Are there scholarships for Sainik School students?
Yes. Scholarships are available from the Ministry of Defence (for defence families), state governments (for SC/ST/OBC), and some schools offer merit-based concessions. Check scholarships.gov.in for details.

### What is the fee for children of army personnel?
Children of serving defence personnel get up to 50% tuition fee waiver. Ex-servicemen's children get up to 25% waiver. War widows and gallantry awardees' children may get up to 75-100% waiver.

### Is mess food included in Sainik School fees?
Yes. Mess charges (₹55,000-65,000 per year) are included in the total fee. The mess provides 3 meals a day plus tea/snacks. Menu is nutritious and planned for growing children.

### Can I pay Sainik School fees in installments?
Most Sainik Schools require full payment before the session starts. Some schools may allow term-wise payment. Check the specific school's fee policy on their website.
