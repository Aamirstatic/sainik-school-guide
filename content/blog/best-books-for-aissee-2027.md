## Sahi Books Ka Selection Kyun Zaroori Hai?

Bahut se parents aur students yeh galti karte hain ki market mein jo bhi book dikhti hai, use khareed lete hain. Lekin **sahi AISSEE preparation books** ka selection isliye critical hai kyunki:

- **Syllabus Coverage:** Har book ka syllabus coverage alag hota hai. Kuch books mein extra topics hote hain jo AISSEE mein nahi aate, aur kuch mein important topics missing hote hain.
- **Difficulty Level:** AISSEE ka difficulty level moderate hota hai. Agar aap bahut tough ya bahut easy book se padhenge, toh exam mein problem hogi.
- **Previous Year Questions:** Acchi books mein pichle saalon ke questions hote hain jo pattern samajhne mein madad karte hain.
- **Time Management:** Sahi book se aapka preparation time optimize hota hai — 3-4 books padhne se behtar hai 1-2 achhi books ko baar-baar revise karna.

AISSEE 2027 ke liye aapko **NCERT textbooks + 1-2 reference books** ka combination chahiye. NCERT base strong karegi, aur reference books exam-specific practice denge.

## Common Book Selection Mistakes

Bahut se students aur parents yeh galtiyan karte hain:

### 1. Bahut Saari Books Khareed Lena

**Problem:** 10-12 books khareed lo aur koi bhi complete nahi karo.

**Solution:** Maximum 2-3 books per subject. Ek NCERT + ek reference book kaafi hai.

### 2. NCERT Ko Ignore Karna

**Problem:** NCERT boring lagti hai, seedha reference books se shuru karte hain.

**Solution:** AISSEE ka 60-70% content directly ya indirectly NCERT se aata hai. NCERT base zaroori hai.

### 3. Latest Edition Na Lena

**Problem:** Purani edition use karna jisme updated syllabus ya current affairs nahi hain.

**Solution:** 2026-2027 edition khareedein, especially GK aur Year Book ke liye.

### 4. Online Reviews Par Blindly Trust Karna

**Problem:** Amazon/Flipkart ke reviews dekh ke books khareedna bina samjhe ki woh book kis level ke liye hai.

**Solution:** Yeh article padh rahe ho — yeh kaafi hai! Expert recommendations follow karo.

### 5. Ek Hi Book Se Sab Subjects Padhna

**Problem:** Ek combined guide se saare subjects padhna.

**Solution:** Combined guides overview ke liye acchi hain, lekin detailed preparation ke liye subject-specific books chahiye.

### 6. Books Padhna But Practice Na Karna

**Problem:** Sirf padho, questions solve na karo.

**Solution:** Har chapter ke baad minimum 50 MCQ solve karo. AISSEE mein speed matter karti hai — practice se hi speed aayegi.

## FAQs

### 1. AISSEE 2027 ke liye kaunsi books best hain?

**Best books for AISSEE 2027** ke liye NCERT textbooks (Class 6 ya Class 9) ko base banaao, phir Arihant ya Upkar ki AISSEE guide se exam-specific practice karo. Mathematics ke liye R.S. Aggarwal, English ke liye Wren & Martin, aur GK ke liye Lucent's GK best hai.

### 2. Kya sirf NCERT se AISSEE crack ho sakta hai?

NCERT se 60-70% syllabus cover ho jaata hai, lekin sirf NCERT se AISSEE crack karna mushkil hai kyunki exam pattern aur question types samajhne ke liye ek dedicated AISSEE guide zaroori hai. NCERT + ek reference book ka combination best hai.

### 3. Class 6 aur Class 9 ke liye alag books chahiye kya?

Haan, Class 6 aur Class 9 ka syllabus alag hota hai. Class 6 mein 4 subjects hain aur Class 9 mein 5 subjects. Dono ke liye separate books khareedni chahiye. Lekin Reasoning ki book (R.S. Aggarwal) ek hi kaam aa sakti hai.

### 4. AISSEE preparation books ki total cost kitni hogi?

Budget option mein Class 6 ke liye ₹700-900 aur Class 9 ke liye ₹1,000-1,400 lagega. Premium option mein Class 6 ke liye ₹1,500-2,000 aur Class 9 ke liye ₹2,200-3,000. Free NCERT PDFs download karke cost aur bhi kam ho sakti hai.

### 5. Kya purane edition ke books chal jayenge?

Mathematics, English, aur Reasoning ke liye purani edition chal jayegi. Lekin GK aur Current Affairs ke liye latest edition (2026-2027) zaroor khareedein. Science aur SST mein bhi agar syllabus change hua hai toh latest edition lena better hai.

### 6. Online books (PDF) ya physical books — kaunsi better hain?

Physical books better hain kyunki:
- Aankhon pe kam strain hota hai
- Notes likhna easy hota hai
- Distraction nahi hota (phone/notifications)
- Revision easy hota hai

Lekin agar budget tight hai toh NCERT ki free PDFs download karo aur reference books physical khareedo.

### 7. Sainik School entrance exam books online kahan se khareedein?

Amazon, Flipkart, aur Arihant ke official website se books khareed sakte hain. Offline local book store se bhi mil jaayengi. Tip: Amazon pe "AISSEE 2027" search karo aur reviews dekh ke khareedo.

### 8. Books kab tak complete kar leni chahiye?

Ideally, exam se **3-4 mahine pehle** saari books complete kar leni chahiye. Last 3 mahine sirf revision, mock tests, aur previous year papers ke liye hone chahiye. Agar exam January mein hai toh September tak books complete karo.
