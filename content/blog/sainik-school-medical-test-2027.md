# Sainik School Medical Test 2027: Height, Weight & Vision Requirements

**Har saal AISSEE qualify karne ke baad lagbhag 15-20% bacche medical test mein fail ho jaate hain.** Yeh shocking isliye hai kyunki zyaada tar parents aur students medical test ko seriously nahi lete. AISSEE crack karna sirf aadhi ladhai hai — baaki aadhi medical fitness hai.

> **Key Takeaway:** Sainik School medical test AISSEE exam qualify karne ke baad hota hai. Height-weight chart age-wise hai (boys aur girls ke liye alag). Minimum vision 6/6 hona chahiye. Common disqualification reasons hain flat foot, knock knee, colour blindness, aur hearing issues. Pehle se preparation zaroori hai.

Yeh guide aapko medical test ka complete breakdown degi — kya check hota hai, kya standards hain, kya disqualify kar sakta hai, aur kaise prepare karna hai.

### Height-Weight Relationship

Sirf height ya weight hona kaafi nahi — **BMI (Body Mass Index)** bhi check hota hai:

| BMI Category | Status |
|-------------|--------|
| Below 15 | Underweight — may be rejected |
| 15-18.5 | Normal for children |
| 18.5-25 | Ideal range |

**Agar baccha thoda underweight ya overweight hai toh:**
- **Underweight:** Balanced diet, protein-rich food, regular exercise
- **Overweight:** Controlled diet, daily running, reduce junk food

## Vision Standards — Detailed Requirements

**Vision test medical test ka sabse important part hai.** Bahut bacche yahan fail hote hain.

### Minimum Vision Requirements

| Test | Minimum Standard | Notes |
|------|-----------------|-------|
| Distant Vision (without glasses) | 6/6 in both eyes | Perfect vision required |
| Distant Vision (with glasses) | 6/6 | Glasses allowed but natural 6/6 preferred |
| Near Vision | N-5 in both eyes | Reading vision |
| Colour Vision | Normal (CP-III minimum) | No colour blindness |
| Binocular Vision | Must be present | Both eyes must work together |

### Common Vision Problems & Their Impact

| Vision Problem | Status | Acceptable? |
|---------------|--------|-------------|
| Myopia (nearsightedness) | -0.5 to -1.5 D | Borderline — may be accepted |
| Myopia (high) | Above -2.0 D | Likely rejection |
| Hypermetropia (farsightedness) | +1.0 to +2.0 D | Borderline |
| Astigmatism | Above 1.0 D | Likely rejection |
| Colour Blindness (CP-IV or worse) | Complete | Rejection |
| Squint (strabismus) | Any | Rejection |
| Night Blindness | Any | Rejection |

### Tips for Vision Preparation

1. **Regular eye check-up** — AISSEE exam se pehle eye test karwayein
2. **Screen time limit** — Phone/computer kam use karein
3. **Eye exercises** — Daily palming, eye rotation exercises
4. **Vitamin A rich food** — Gajar, palak, amla, eggs
5. **Proper sleep** — 8-9 hours ki neend zaroori hai
6. **Outdoor activities** — Din mein 1-2 hours bahut zaroori hai

## Complete Medical Test Components

Medical test mein yeh sab check hota hai:

### 1. General Physical Examination

| Component | What They Check | Common Issues |
|-----------|----------------|---------------|
| Height | Measured with stadiometer | Short height for age |
| Weight | Measured on calibrated scale | Underweight/Overweight |
| BMI | Calculated from height & weight | Abnormal BMI |
| Chest | Expanded & unexpanded measurement | Underdeveloped chest |
| Pulse | Rate & rhythm | Irregular pulse |
| Blood Pressure | Systolic & diastolic | High/low BP |

### 2. Orthopedic Examination (Bones & Joints)

**Yeh bahut important hai — yahan sabse zyaada rejection hota hai.**

| Condition | Status | Details |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| **Flat Foot (Flat pair)** | ❌ Rejection | Arch of foot absent |
| **Knock Knee (Knock pair)** | ❌ Rejection | Knees touch when standing straight |
| **Bow Legs** | ❌ Rejection | Legs curved outward |
| **Bent Spine** | ❌ Rejection | Scoliosis or kyphosis |
| **Surgical History** | Case by case | Major surgeries may disqualify |
| **Fracture History** | Case by case | Healed fractures usually OK |
| **Joint Mobility** | Must be normal | Restricted movement = rejection |

### 3. ENT (Ear, Nose, Throat)

| Test | Standard | Common Issues |
|------|----------|---------------|
| Hearing | Whisper test at 6 meters | Hearing loss |
| Ear Drum | Intact, no perforation | Hole in eardrum |
| Nose | No deviated septum | DNS (Deviated Nasal Septum) |
| Throat | No tonsils enlargement | Enlarged tonsils |
| Wax | No excessive wax | Ear wax blockage |

### 4. Eye Examination (Detailed)

| Test | Method | Standard |
|------|--------|----------|
| Distant Vision | Snellen Chart | 6/6 both eyes |
| Near Vision | Jaeger Chart | N-5 both eyes |
| Colour Vision | Ishihara Plates | CP-III minimum |
| Field of Vision | Confrontation test | Full field |
| Fundoscopy | Ophthalmoscope | Normal retina |

### 5. Cardiovascular

| Test | Standard |
|------|----------|
| Heart Rate | 60-100 bpm (normal) |
| Heart Murmur | No abnormal sounds |
| ECG (if needed) | Normal rhythm |

### 6. Respiratory System

| Test | Standard |
|------|----------|
| Chest X-ray | Normal lungs |
| Breathing | No wheezing/asthma |
| TB Screening | Negative |

### 7. Dental Examination

| Test | Standard |
|------|----------|
| Teeth Count | Minimum 14 dental points |
| Cavities | Should be treated |
| Gum Health | Healthy gums |
| Missing Teeth | May cause rejection |

### 8. Laboratory Tests

| Test | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Urine Routine | Kidney function, infections |
| Blood Routine | Haemoglobin, WBC, RBC |
| Blood Sugar | Diabetes screening |

## How to Prepare for Medical Test — Complete Guide

### 3-Month Preparation Plan

**Month 1: Assessment**
- Complete health check-up karwayein
- Eye test karwayein — vision check
- Dental check-up karwayein
- Height-weight measure karein
- Identify potential issues

**Month 2: Correction**
- Diet plan follow karein (weight ke according)
- Eye exercises start karein
- Dental treatment complete karein
- Physical exercise routine set karein
- Flat foot exercises (if mild)

**Month 3: Maintenance**
- Continue diet & exercise
- Recheck weight & height
- Final eye test
- Final dental check
- Mental preparation

### Diet Plan for Weight Management

| Meal | Underweight Child | Overweight Child |
|------|-------------------|------------------|
| Breakfast | Milk, eggs, banana, paratha | Milk (toned), fruits, oats |
| Mid-morning | Dry fruits, banana | Sprouts, salad |
| Lunch | Rice, dal, sabzi, chicken/egg | Roti, dal, sabzi (less oil) |
| Evening | Milkshake, sandwich | Green tea, biscuits (2 only) |
| Dinner | Roti, paneer/chicken, curd | Roti, dal, salad |
| Before bed | Warm milk with turmeric | Nothing (eat 3 hrs before bed) |

### Physical Exercise Routine

| Day | Activity | Duration |
|-----|----------|----------|
| Monday | Running + Stretching | 45 min |
| Tuesday | Bodyweight exercises | 40 min |
| Wednesday | Sports (any) | 1 hour |
| Thursday | Running + Core exercises | 45 min |
| Friday | Flexibility + Yoga | 40 min |
| Saturday | Full PT routine | 1 hour |
| Sunday | Rest / Light walk | 30 min |

### Eye Exercises for Vision Improvement

1. **Palming:** Rub hands, place over closed eyes, relax 2 min
2. **Eye Rotation:** Clockwise 10 times, anti-clockwise 10 times
3. **Focus Shifting:** Focus on near object (10 sec), far object (10 sec), repeat 10 times
4. **Blinking:** Rapid blinking 20 times, rest, repeat 3 sets
5. **Figure 8:** Trace figure 8 with eyes, 10 times each direction

## What Happens After Medical Test?

### Three Possible Outcomes

| Outcome | What It Means | Next Step |
|---------|--------------|-----------|
| **Fit** | Passed all tests | Eligible for final merit list |
| **Temporary Unfit** | Minor issues — can be corrected | Given time to re-appear |
| **Permanently Unfit** | Cannot be corrected | Not eligible for admission |

### Appeal Process

Agar aapko lagta hai medical test galat hua hai:
1. Written appeal school authorities ko submit karein
2. Re-examination ki request karein
3. Higher medical board ke paas ja sakte hain
4. **Time limit:** Usually 15-30 days mein appeal karna hota hai

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Medical test kab hota hai Sainik School ke liye?

Medical test **AISSEE exam qualify karne ke baad** hota hai — usually March-April mein. Sirf qualifying candidates ko bulaya jaata hai.

### Kitna weight hona chahiye Sainik School ke liye?

Weight age-wise check hota hai. 12 saal ke bacche ke liye approximately **30-47 kg** hona chahiye (boys), **29-45 kg** (girls). Exact chart upar diya gaya hai.

### Kya flat foot se rejection hota hai?

**Haan, flat foot ek common reason hai rejection ka.** Agar bachpan se flat foot hai toh correction mushkil hai. Mild cases mein special insoles se manage ho sakta hai.

### Kitni eyesight chahiye Sainik School ke liye?

**Minimum 6/6 vision** dono aankhon mein chahiye. Mild myopia (-0.5 to -1.5 D) borderline hai — accept bhi ho sakta hai, reject bhi. Glasses allowed hain lekin natural vision preferred hai.

### Kya colour blindness se rejection hota hai?

**Haan, CP-IV ya worse colour vision se rejection hota hai.** CP-III (mild) acceptable hai.

### Medical test mein kaunse documents le jaane hain?

AISSEE admit card, result, birth certificate, Aadhaar, category certificate, photographs, medical fitness certificate — sab original aur photocopy le jaana hai.

### Kya medical test ke liye preparation kar sakte hain?

**Haan, bahut kuch prepare kiya ja sakta hai.** Weight management, dental treatment, eye exercises, general fitness — sab 3-6 months mein improve ho sakta hai.

### Medical test fail hone pe kya karna hai?

Temporary unfit mila hai toh correction ke baad re-appear ho sakte hain. Permanently unfit mila hai toh appeal process hai — written appeal aur re-examination ki request kar sakte hain.
