India ke Sainik Schools defence forces ke liye young leaders tayar karte hain. Agar aap apne bacche ka admission Sainik School mein karana chahte hain, toh yeh guide aapke liye hai. ## Sainik Schools Kya Hain?

Sainik Schools Society (Ministry of Defence) ke under 33+ Sainik Schools poore India mein operate karte hain. Yeh schools students ko NDA (National Defence Academy) aur other defence services ke liye tayar karte hain. Har saal AISSEE (All India Sainik Schools Entrance Exam) ke through Class 6 aur Class 9 mein admission hota hai.

Aap [AISSEE 2027 ki complete guide](/aissee/) yahan padh sakte hain. Saare Sainik Schools ki list ke liye [Sainik Schools directory](/sainik-schools/) visit karein.

## Admission Process

### Eligibility Criteria

**For Class 6:**
- Age: 10-12 years as on 31st March of admission year
- Class 5 passed from recognized school

**For Class 9:**
- Age: 13-15 years as on 31st March of admission year
- Class 8 passed from recognized school

### Selection Process

1. **AISSEE Written Exam** - [AISSEE preparation guide](/aissee/) padhein
2. **Medical Test** - Physical fitness check
3. **Merit List** - Based on written exam + medical
4. **Document Verification**
5. **Final Admission**

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Sainik School ki fees kitni hoti hai?
Average fees ₹90,000 se ₹1,50,000 per year hoti hai. Defence wards aur SC/ST students ko scholarship milti hai. Har school ki fees alag hoti hai - official website se confirm karein.

### Kya Sainik School sirf boys ke liye hai?
Nahi! Ab kuch Sainik Schools mein girls ka bhi admission hota hai. Government ne girls ke liye bhi seats open ki hain. Latest information ke liye [AISSEE page](/aissee/) check karein.

### Sainik School se NDA mein selection chances kitne hain?
Sainik School students ka NDA selection rate regular schools se kaafi high hai. Top schools mein 15-25% students NDA mein select hote hain. Overall Sainik Schools ka contribution NDA mein bahut significant hai.

### Best Sainik School kaunsa hai?
Overall ranking mein Korukonda, Kunjpura, aur Chittorgarh top 3 mein aate hain. Lekin best school aapke liye woh hai jo aapki location, budget, aur child ke needs ko suit kare.

### Admission ke liye coaching zaruri hai?
Coaching zaruri nahi hai, lekin structured preparation zaruri hai. Self-study se bhi AISSEE crack kiya ja sakta hai. [AISSEE preparation tips](/aissee/) padhein detailed strategy ke liye.

### Sainik School mein kya milta hai?
Sainik School mein CBSE education, military-style discipline, sports training, NCC, aur overall personality development milta hai. Yeh schools NDA aur defence forces ke liye leaders tayar karte hain.

### Medical test mein kya hota hai?
Medical test mein physical fitness, vision, hearing, aur overall health check hota hai. Height-weight proportion, flat foot check, aur dental examination bhi hota hai.

### Kya Sainik School se civilian career bhi ban sakta hai?
Haan! Sainik School alumni bahut successful civilians bhi hain - IAS officers, doctors, engineers, businessmen. School sirf defence nahi, overall character build karta hai.
