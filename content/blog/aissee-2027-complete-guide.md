## AISSEE Kya Hai?

AISSEE ka full form hai **All India Sainik Schools Entrance Examination**. Yeh exam **National Testing Agency (NTA)** ke dwara conduct kiya jaata hai aur India bhar mein 33 Sainik Schools mein admission ke liye hota hai.

Sainik Schools ka establishment **1961** mein hua tha — Pt. Jawaharlal Nehru ji ke vision se — jiska aim tha ki country ke brightest young minds ko military leadership ke liye taiyaar kiya jaaye. Aaj Sainik Schools India ke most prestigious residential schools mein count hote hain.

### Key Highlights

- **Conducting Body:** National Testing Agency (NTA)
- **Exam Level:** National
- **Frequency:** Once a year
- **Mode:** OMR-based (pen and paper)
- **Classes Offered:** Class 6 and Class 9
- **Total Sainik Schools:** 33 (including new ones under the Vision 2024 expansion)
- **Official Website:** [https://exams.nta.ac.in/AISSEE/](https://exams.nta.ac.in/AISSEE/)

Sainik School se pass out hone wale students ko **NDA (National Defence Academy)** aur **Indian Armed Forces** mein directly jaane ka ek strong foundation milta hai. Statistics batate hain ki Sainik School alumni ka NDA selection rate national average se **3 guna zyada** hai.

## Subject-wise Preparation Tips

### 1. Mathematics — "Practice Makes Perfect"

Mathematics mein score karna sabse zyada important hai kyunki iska weightage maximum hai.

**Daily Routine:**
- **1.5-2 hours** daily Maths practice karein
- Pehle concepts samjhein, phir speed badhaayein
- **Previous year papers** solve karein — pattern repeat hota hai

**Key Strategy:**
- Tables (2-20), squares (1-30), cubes (1-15) yaad karein
- Percentage ↔ Fraction conversions rat lein (e.g., 1/8 = 12.5%)
- Word problems daily 10 solve karein
- Geometry mein diagrams banakar practice karein

**Common Mistakes:**
- Silly calculation errors — rough work neat rakhein
- Time waste on tough questions — pehle easy wale solve karein

### 2. General Knowledge — "Daily Reading Habit"

GK ek aisa subject hai jo daily consistent reading se improve hota hai.

**Daily Routine:**
- **30 minutes** newspaper reading (Hindi ya English)
- **Weekly** ek GK magazine padhein
- Science aur Social Science ke **NCERT textbooks** thoroughly padhein

**Key Strategy:**
- Science: Focus on "why" — ratta mat maaro, concepts samjho
- Social Science: Timelines banayein for History, Maps use karein for Geography
- Current Affairs: Last 6 months ke important events cover karein

### 3. Reasoning — "Pattern Recognition"

Reasoning section relatively scoring hai agar aap patterns samajh lein.

**Daily Routine:**
- **45 minutes** daily reasoning practice
- Har type ka 5 questions daily solve karein

**Key Strategy:**
- Pehle basic types master karein — series, analogy, classification
- Visual reasoning ke liye practice material use karein
- Speed badhaane ke liye timed practice karein

### 4. Language — "Read, Read, Read"

Language section mein reading comprehension aur grammar dono aate hain.

**Daily Routine:**
- **30 minutes** reading practice (stories, articles)
- Grammar rules weekly revise karein

**Key Strategy:**
- Vocabulary build karein — daily 5 naye words seekhein
- Comprehension passages practice karein — questions pehle padhein, phir passage
- Essay writing ki practice karein — 100-150 words mein

## FAQs

### 1. AISSEE 2027 ka exam kab hoga?

AISSEE 2027 ka exam **January 2027** mein expected hai (pehle ya doosre Sunday par). Official date NTA ki website par notification ke baad confirm hogi. Regularly [NTA ki official website](https://exams.nta.ac.in/AISSEE/) check karte rahein.

### 2. AISSEE 2027 ke liye application form kab se available hoga?

Application form **October 2026** mein release hone ki sambhavna hai. Last year ka pattern dekhein toh October-November ke beech application window khulti hai. Is page ko bookmark karein taaki aapko update milta rahe.

### 3. Kya girls bhi AISSEE 2027 ke liye apply kar sakti hain?

Haan! Ab zyaadatar Sainik Schools mein **co-education** shuru ho chuka hai. Girls Class 6 aur Class 9 dono ke liye eligible hain. Lekin kuch schools mein seats limited hain, toh check karein ki aapke preferred school mein kitni seats hain.

### 4. AISSEE mein negative marking hoti hai?

**Nahi.** AISSEE mein koi negative marking nahi hai. Iska matlab hai ki agar aapko answer nahi pata, toh bhi guess karke attempt karna chahiye — koi penalty nahi lagegi.

### 5. AISSEE 2027 ki preparation kab se shuru karein?

Ideally, aapko **6-8 months pehle** se preparation shuru kar deni chahiye. Agar exam January 2027 mein hai, toh **June-July 2026** se start karein. Daily 3-4 hours consistent study kaafi hai. Agar late ho gaye hai, toh focused 3-month plan se bhi crack ho sakta hai — bas discipline chahiye.

### 6. AISSEE ke liye best coaching kya hai?

Aaj ke time mein online coaching bahut effective ho gayi hai. Options hain:
- **Self-study** (NCERT + Guide Books) — agar baccha disciplined hai
- **Online courses** — YouTube channels, Unacademy, Adda247
- **Offline coaching** — local coaching centres (agar available ho)

Meri recommendation: **Pehle self-study try karein** — NCERT + ek achhi guide book + previous year papers. Agar 2-3 months mein progress na dikhe, tab coaching join karein.

### 7. Sainik School ki fees kitni hai?

Sainik School ki fees **₹1,00,000 - ₹1,50,000 per year** approximately hai (boarding, tuition, aur other charges milakar). Lekin SC/ST candidates ko **fee concession** milta hai. Defence category ke liye bhi special provisions hain. Har school ki fees alag hoti hai — school ki website par check karein.

### 8. AISSEE mein kitne questions aate hain?

- **Class 6:** 125 questions (300 marks)
- **Class 9:** 150 questions (400 marks)

Sabhi questions Multiple Choice (MCQ) type ke hain. Exam OMR sheet par hota hai — pen and paper mode.

### 9. AISSEE crack karne ke baad kya hota hai?

AISSEE crack karne ke baad:
1. **Medical Test** hota hai — physical fitness check
2. **Merit List** based on exam marks + medical fitness
3. **Counselling** process mein school allotment hota hai
4. **Admission** milta hai allotted Sainik School mein

Sainik School se pass out hone ke baad students ko **NDA, Indian Military Academy, Naval Academy, Air Force Academy** mein jaane ka strong foundation milta hai.

### 10. Kya AISSEE 2027 ke liye koi reservation hai?

Haan, AISSEE mein category-wise reservation hai:
- **SC:** 15%
- **ST:** 7.5%
- **OBC (Non-Creamy Layer):** 27%
- **Defence Category:** Up to 25% seats (school-specific)
- **Home State Quota:** 67% seats domicile candidates ke liye
