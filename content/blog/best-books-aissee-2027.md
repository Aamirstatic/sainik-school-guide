## AISSEE 2027 Books: You Don't Need 10 Books. You Need the Right 5.

Parents often buy every Sainik School guide they can find. The child ends up with 15 books, finishes none of them, and scores average marks. That is a waste of money and time.

For AISSEE 2027, you need exactly 4-5 books per class. NCERT is the foundation. One guide book for practice. One for GK. One for reasoning. That is it. This guide tells you which ones actually work, based on what previous toppers used.

## Books for Class 6 AISSEE 2027

Class 6 has 125 questions for 300 marks across four subjects. Here is what you need.

### Mathematics

| Book | Author/Publisher | Price (Approx.) | Why This Book |
|------|-----------------|-----------------|---------------|
| NCERT Mathematics Class 5 | NCERT | ₹30-50 | Covers 80% of the syllabus. Free PDF available. |
| Sainik School Entrance Exam Guide (Class 6) | Arihant | ₹200-300 | Previous year papers + practice questions |
| Mathematics for Sainik School | Upkar Prakashan | ₹150-200 | Extra practice for weak topics |

Start with NCERT. Solve every exercise. Only then move to the guide book. Most questions in AISSEE come directly from NCERT-level concepts.

### General Knowledge

| Book | Author/Publisher | Price (Approx.) | Why This Book |
|------|-----------------|-----------------|---------------|
| Lucent's General Knowledge | Lucent | ₹200-300 | Best static GK book in India. Covers history, geography, science. |
| Pratiyogita Darpan (Monthly) | PD Group | ₹50/month | Current affairs updates |

Lucent is a one-book solution for static GK. Read 10 pages daily. For current affairs, read a newspaper or use a free GK app.

### Language (Hindi/English)

| Book | Author/Publisher | Price (Approx.) | Why This Book |
|------|-----------------|-----------------|---------------|
| Wren & Martin High School Grammar | S. Chand | ₹300-400 | Grammar rules explained clearly |
| NCERT Hindi Class 5 | NCERT | ₹30-50 | Hindi comprehension preparation |

### Intelligence/Reasoning

| Book | Author/Publisher | Price (Approx.) | Why This Book |
|------|-----------------|-----------------|---------------|
| Mental Ability Test | R.S. Aggarwal | ₹200-300 | All pattern types covered. Industry standard. |
| Sainik School Reasoning Guide | Arihant | ₹150-200 | AISSEE-specific patterns |

R.S. Aggarwal is the only reasoning book you need. It has 500+ practice questions covering every pattern that appears in AISSEE.

### Total Cost for Class 6 Books

| Category | Books | Cost |
|----------|-------|------|
| Mathematics | NCERT + Arihant | ₹250-350 |
| GK | Lucent | ₹200-300 |
| Language | Wren & Martin + NCERT | ₹330-450 |

Under ₹1,500 for the complete preparation. Do not buy more than this.

## Books You Should NOT Buy

These are a waste of money for AISSEE preparation:

| Book Type | Why to Avoid |
|-----------|-------------|
| "Complete Guide" books with 1000+ pages | Too thick. You will never finish them. |
| Multiple books for the same subject | Confusing. One book per subject is enough. |
| Previous year papers books (old editions) | Pattern has changed. Use only 2021-2026 papers. |
| Expensive coaching material | NCERT + guide book covers the same content for free. |
