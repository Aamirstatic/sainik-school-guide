## Sainik School Admission Process Overview

**Sainik School admission process** ek multi-stage system hai jismein written exam, medical test, aur counselling — teeno phases hote hain. Yeh process **National Testing Agency (NTA)** ke dwara conduct ki jaati hai aur poora India mein centralized hoti hai.

### Key Highlights

| Detail | Information |
|---|---|
| **Conducting Body** | National Testing Agency (NTA) |
| **Exam Name** | AISSEE (All India Sainik Schools Entrance Examination) |
| **Classes Offered** | Class 6 and Class 9 |
| **Total Sainik Schools** | 33+ across India |
| **Exam Mode** | OMR-based (pen and paper) |
| **Application Mode** | Online only |
| **Official Website** | [exams.nta.ac.in/AISSEE](https://exams.nta.ac.in/AISSEE/) |

Sainik School se pass out hone wale students ko **NDA (National Defence Academy)** aur Indian Armed Forces mein jaane ka ek strong foundation milta hai. Statistics batate hain ki Sainik School alumni ka NDA selection rate national average se **3 guna zyada** hai.

## Step-by-Step Admission Guide

**Sainik School admission 2027** ka process nimnalikhit steps mein hota hai:

### Step 1: Online Registration (October-November 2026)

- NTA ki official website par jaayein: [exams.nta.ac.in/AISSEE](https://exams.nta.ac.in/AISSEE/)
- "New Registration" par click karein
- Apna email ID aur mobile number register karein
- Application number aur password generate hoga — ise note kar lein

### Step 2: Application Form Bharna

- Login karein apne application number aur password se
- Personal details, academic details, aur school preferences fill karein
- **3 Sainik Schools** tak preference de sakte hain
- Saari details carefully check karein — baad mein correction ka limited window milta hai

### Step 3: Document Upload

- Passport size photograph (recent, white background)
- Signature scan
- Birth certificate
- Category certificate (agar applicable ho)
- Aapki file size aur format requirements NTA website par diye gaye hain

### Step 4: Fee Payment

| Category | Application Fee |
|---|---|
| General / OBC / EWS | ₹650 (approx.) |
| SC / ST | ₹500 (approx.) |

*Note: Fee amount official notification ke baad confirm hogi.*

### Step 5: Admit Card Download (December 2026)

- Admit card NTA website se download karna hoga
- Exam centre, date, aur time admit card par hoga
- Admit card ke saath ek valid photo ID bhi le jaana zaroori hai

### Step 6: Appear for AISSEE (January 2027)

- Exam generally January ke first ya second Sunday ko hota hai
- Class 6 ke liye exam duration: **2 hours 30 minutes**
- Class 9 ke liye exam duration: **3 hours**
- OMR sheet par answers mark karne hote hain

### Step 7: Result Declaration (February-March 2027)

- NTA website par result check karein
- Scorecard download karein
- Qualifying candidates ko next stage ke liye bulaya jaata hai

### Step 8: Medical Test (March-April 2027)

- Medical fitness test Sainik School mein hota hai
- Height, weight, eyesight, aur general health check hota hai
- Medical standards Indian Armed Forces ke norms par based hain

### Step 9: Final Merit List & Counselling (April-June 2027)

- Written exam marks + medical test ke basis par merit list banti hai
- Counselling ke through school allotment hota hai
- Seat accept karne ke baad admission confirm hota hai

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

Bahut saare parents kuch common galtiyan karte hain jo admission process mein problem create karti hain. Inhe avoid karein:

### 1. Age Criteria Galat Samajhna

Bahut parents age calculation mein galti karte hain. **31 March 2027** ki date ko base maanke age calculate karein — exam date nahi.

### 2. Documents Deri Se Banana

Birth certificate ya domicile certificate banane mein time lagta hai. **Form bharne se 2-3 mahine pehle** saari documents ready kar lein.

### 3. Sirf Ek School Preference Dena

Form mein **3 school preferences** dena allowed hai — sirf 1 mat dein. Apne state ke saath doosre states ke schools bhi choose karein.

### 4. Medical Test Ki Preparation Na Karna

Written exam ke baad medical test hota hai. Bahut students written exam clear karte hain lekin medical mein reject ho jaate hain. **Eyesight, weight, aur height** ka dhyan rakhein.

### 5. Last Date Par Form Bharna

Last date par NTA website par traffic bahut zyada hota hai — server slow ho jaata hai ya crash bhi ho sakta hai. **Kam se kam 1 hafte pehle** form bharein.

### 6. Category Certificate Ki Validity Check Na Karna

OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) aur EWS certificates ki **validity period** hoti hai. Expired certificate upload karein toh form reject ho jaayega.

### 7. Counselling Mein Na Jaana

Written exam clear karne ke baad counselling mein **personally jaana** zaroori hai. Agar nahi jaate toh seat allot nahi hogi.

## FAQs

### 1. Sainik School admission 2027 ke liye form kab bharne honge?

**Sainik School admission 2027** ke liye online applications expected **October-November 2026** mein shuru hongi. Official notification NTA ki website [exams.nta.ac.in/AISSEE](https://exams.nta.ac.in/AISSEE/) par aayegi.

### 2. Kya girls bhi Sainik School mein admission le sakti hain?

Haan, **2024 se Sainik Schools mein girls ka admission bhi hota hai** Class 6 mein. Yeh ek positive change hai aur ab boys aur girls dono ke liye seats available hain.

### 3. Sainik School admission 2027 ke liye age kitni honi chahiye?

Class 6 ke liye **10-12 years** aur Class 9 ke liye **13-15 years** (as on 31 March 2027). Age calculation mein galti na karein — yeh sabse common reason hai form reject hone ka.

### 4. AISSEE exam ka pattern kya hai?

Class 6 ke liye **Maths, GK, Language, aur Intelligence** — total **125 questions** aur **2.5 hours** ka time. Class 9 ke liye **Maths, Science, English, Social Studies, aur Intelligence** — total **150 questions** aur **3 hours** ka time. Detail ke liye [AISSEE 2027 complete guide](/blog/aissee-2027-complete-guide/) padhein.

### 5. Sainik School ki fees kitni hai?

Annual fees **₹1,00,000 - ₹1,57,000** ke beech hoti hai (General category ke liye). SC/ST students ko **50% tak concession** milta hai. Har school ki exact fees alag hoti hai — official website se confirm karein.

### 6. Kya Sainik School admission ke liye coaching zaroori hai?

Coaching zaroori nahi hai — bahut saare students bina coaching ke bhi clear karte hain. Lekin **structured preparation**, NCERT books, aur previous year papers solve karna zaroori hai. [Sainik School preparation tips](/blog/aissee-2027-preparation-tips/) yahan padhein.

### 7. Medical test mein kya check hota hai?

Medical test mein **height, weight, eyesight, hearing, dental health, aur general physical fitness** check hota hai. Standards Indian Armed Forces ke norms par based hain. Written exam clear karne ke baad yeh mandatory hai.

### 8. Agar ek baar form reject ho jaaye toh kya karein?

Form rejection ke common reasons hain: galat age, expired category certificate, ya galat photo format. **Rejection ke baad aap dubara form bhar sakte hain** agar last date na nikli ho. NTA helpline par bhi call kar sakte hain.
