    Sainik School for Girls 2027: Complete Admission Guide for Girl Students

    2021 mein ek historic decision liya gaya — Sainik Schools ne pehli baar girls ko admission dena shuru kiya. Aur sirf 4 saal mein, har Sainik School mein girl cadets hain jo boys ke saath kandhe se kandha milakar march karti hain.

    Key Takeaway: Sainik Schools mein girls ke liye Class 6 mein 10% seats reserved hain. Admission process bilkul same hai — AISSEE exam dena hoga, same eligibility criteria hain, aur fee structure bhi same hai. Ab 33+ Sainik Schools mein girl students padh rahi hain.

    Kya aapki beti Sainik School join karna chahti hai? Yeh guide aapko har cheez batayegi — eligibility se lekar hostel facilities tak, success stories se lekar application process tak.

    Sainik Schools Mein Girls Admission Ki History

    Sainik Schools ki establishment 1961 mein hui thi — tab se lekar 2021 tak 60 saal tak sirf boys allowed the. Lekin National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 aur Ministry of Defence ke ek bold decision ne sab badal diya.

    Can Girls Join Sainik School? — The Definitive Answer

    Haan, girls bilkul Sainik School join kar sakti hain. Yeh ab koi question nahi hai. Lekin kuch specific rules hain jo aapko samajhne chahiye:

    Girls Ke Liye Reserved Seats

    • Class 6: Total sanctioned seats ka minimum 10% girls ke liye reserved hai
    • Class 9: Kuch schools mein Class 9 mein bhi girls admission hota hai, lekin limited seats
    • New Sainik Schools (under PPP mode): Inme bhi girls ke liye seats hain

    Kya AISSEE Exam Alag Hota Hai Girls Ke Liye?

    Bilkul nahi. Girls aur boys ke liye:

    • ✅ Same AISSEE exam — same paper, same difficulty level
    • ✅ Same syllabus — Mathematics, English, Intelligence, Social Science, Hindi/Regional Language
    • ✅ Same marking scheme — OMR-based, 2.5 marks per correct answer (Class 6)
    • ✅ Same eligibility criteria — age limit, educational qualification
    • ✅ Same fee structure — koi extra charges nahi girls ke liye

    Bas ek difference hai — merit list alag banegi girls ke liye, kyunki reserved seats hain.

    Medical Fitness

    Girls ko bhi medical test pass karna hoga. Medical standards mein kuch minor differences hain:

    • Height & Weight: Girls ke liye separate chart hai (age-wise)
    • Vision: Same as boys — 6/6 minimum
    • Other tests: Hearing, dental, general physical fitness — sab same hai

    New Sainik Schools (Under PPP Mode)

    Government ne 100+ new Sainik Schools approve kiye hain Public-Private Partnership mode mein. Inme bhi girls ke liye seats available hain. Yeh schools existing private schools ke infrastructure mein operate karte hain lekin Sainik School curriculum follow karte hain.

    Girl-Specific Considerations: Hostel, Safety & Facilities

    Parents ka sabse bada concern — “Meri beti ka hostel mein kaise hoga?” Yeh bahut valid concern hai. Let me address this with facts.

    Hostel Facilities for Girls

    Sainik Schools mein girls ke liye separate hostel blocks banaye gaye hain:

    • Separate building/wing — boys hostel se completely alag
    • Dedicated warden — female warden 24/7 hostel mein rehti hai
    • CCTV surveillance — hostel premises mein cameras
    • Restricted entry — boys ka entry strictly prohibited in girls’ hostel
    • Common room — separate common room with TV, books, games
    • Washrooms — attached/separate washroom facilities

    Safety Measures

    Safety FeatureDetails
    Female StaffDedicated female teachers and support staff
    Warden SystemFemale warden residing in hostel 24/7
    CCTVComplete coverage of hostel and common areas
    Entry/ExitBiometric/manual register maintained
    MedicalLady doctor/nurse available on campus
    Complaint SystemAnonymous complaint mechanism for girls
    Anti-RaggingStrict anti-ragging policy with separate monitoring

    Medical Facilities

    • Lady doctor ya female nurse har campus pe available hai
    • Separate medical room for girls in hostel
    • Monthly health check-ups
    • Mental health counseling — trained counselors available
    • Menstrual hygiene — adequate supply and private facilities

    AISSEE 2027 Application Process for Girls

    Step-by-Step Process

    Step 1: Online Registration

    • Official website pe jaayein: exams.nta.ac.in/AISSEE
    • “New Registration” pe click karein
    • Apna email ID aur mobile number register karein

    Step 2: Fill Application Form

    • Personal details bharen
    • Category select karein (General/SC/ST/OBC)
    • Gender: Female select karein
    • School preference bharen (top 3 choices)

    Step 3: Upload Documents

    • Recent passport-size photograph
    • Signature
    • Birth certificate
    • Category certificate (if applicable)

    Step 4: Pay Application Fee

    • General/OBC: ₹550
    • SC/ST: ₹400
    • Payment online (debit card/credit card/net banking/UPI)

    Step 5: Submit & Print

    • Form submit karein
    • Confirmation page print karke rakhein

    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

    Kya girls Sainik School mein admission le sakti hain?

    Haan, bilkul. 2021 se sabhi Sainik Schools mein girls ke liye 10% seats reserved hain. Girls AISSEE exam dekar admission le sakti hain.

    Kya girls ke liye alag exam hota hai?

    Nahi. Girls aur boys ka AISSEE exam ek hi hota hai — same paper, same syllabus, same marking scheme. Bas merit list alag banegi girls ke liye.

    Girls ke liye hostel kaisa hai?

    Girls ke liye separate hostel hota hai boys se alag. Female warden 24/7 rehti hai, CCTV surveillance hota hai, aur complete safety measures hain.

    Fee girls ke liye zyada hai kya?

    Nahi. Fee structure girls aur boys ke liye same hai. Koi extra charges nahi hain.

    Kya girls NDA join kar sakti hain Sainik School ke baad?

    Haan! 2021 se girls bhi NDA exam de sakti hain. Sainik School ki training NDA ke liye strong foundation deti hai. Girls ko bhi SSB preparation aur NDA coaching milti hai.

    Kitni seats hain girls ke liye?

    Har Sainik School mein approximately 10-20 seats girls ke liye reserved hain (school ke total sanctioned seats ka minimum 10%). Exact number har saal official notification mein milta hai.

    Kya Class 9 mein bhi girls ka admission hota hai?

    Haan, kuch schools mein Class 9 mein bhi girls admission hota hai, lekin seats bahut limited hain. Zyaada tar seats Class 6 ke liye hain.

    Medical test mein kya alag hai girls ke liye?

    Height-weight chart alag hai girls ke liye (age-wise). Baaki tests — vision, hearing, dental — sab same hai. Detail ke liye medical test guide padhein.
